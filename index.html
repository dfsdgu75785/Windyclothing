Our Story

Born From
The Island

WINDY was born on the streets of Kurunegala — where the monsoon winds carry stories, where temples meet traffic, where ancient pattern meets modern pace.

We take Sri Lanka's visual heritage — the kolam patterns, the batik weave, the hand-block prints — and thread them into streetwear that moves with you, not against you.

Every piece is limited, intentional, and rooted. This isn't fashion. This is a conversation between the old island and the new generation.
